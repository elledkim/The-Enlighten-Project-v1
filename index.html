The Enlighten Project

We believe education should be easily accessible to all youth.

The Enlighten Project is a youth-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing the lives of underprivileged youth in youth shelters, juvenile detention centers, and impoverished neighborhoods through improved access to education and other necessities.

Our Mission

Since 2023, we have led the effort to ensure that all youth are provided access to educational resources, support networks, and opportunities for higher education.


At The Enlighten Project, our mission is to transform the lives of underprivileged children in youth shelters, juvenile detention facilities, and impoverished neighborhoods through enhanced access to education and other essential resources. We believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive and build a brighter future, regardless of their circumstances.

Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff is passionate about education inequality and its effect on marginalized students. They work tirelessly to create opportunities and empower these children to break the cycle of poverty. We are committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive environment where they can develop their full potential.

We work closely with libraries, community centers, and other organizations to provide resources and other educational opportunities for underprivileged children. We also provide access to essential resources such as food, clothing, and school supplies, which can help children feel more secure and supported.

We also work to raise awareness of the challenges faced by underprivileged children and advocate for policies and programs that support their needs. By building relationships with families and caregivers in underprivileged communities, we aim to provide additional support and resources that can help to improve children's lives. At our core, we believe that every child deserves access to education and the resources they need to succeed. We are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of underprivileged children and to building a brighter future for all youth.

  • Our Partners

Through our initiatives, we aim to:


  • Provide Essential Resources: We recognize that basic necessities are critical for a child's well-being and educational journey. We organize supply drives to ensure children have access to school supplies, clothing, personal hygiene products, and other essentials, enabling them to focus on their education and personal growth.
  • Expand Educational Access: We strive to bridge the educational gap by offering tailored educational programs.
  • Foster Holistic Development: We understand that education is not solely confined to classrooms. Our organization emphasizes the holistic development of each child by offering health and mental wellness support, career development programs, and recreational activities that nurture their talents and aspirations.
  • Advocate for Change: We are passionate advocates for the rights and well-being of underprivileged children. By raising awareness about their challenges and mobilizing support from the community, government, and other stakeholders, we strive to bring about long-term systemic change that addresses the root causes of poverty and educational inequality.
  • Build Stronger Communities: We firmly believe in the power of collaboration and community engagement. By forging partnerships with local libraries, schools, organizations, and families, we create a network of support that uplifts underprivileged children, instills a sense of belonging, and helps them realize their potential as active contributors to society.
